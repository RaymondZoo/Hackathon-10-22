About

Mission Statement

The world’s climate is suffering tremendously due to carbon dioxide trapped in the atmosphere from ICE vehicles. It causes unpredictable weather patterns and severe natural calamities. Additionally emissions from ICE vehicles also lead to many adverse health issues such as carcinogenic chemicals from car exhaust - which greatly increases the risk of certain cancers - and irritants that cause more damage to lungs and airways to pre-existing diseases.

Charities

Ashante Africa Foundation

Asante Africa Foundation is a global team committed to empowering East Africa’s youth. Their mission is to educate the next generation of change agents, whose dreams and actions transform the future for Africa and the world. Asante develops Africa’s youth through leadership. entrepreneurship, and job readiness. Learn more: https://asanteafrica.org/about-us

Charities

Global Partnership for Education

Global Partnership for Education is exclusively dedicated to giving more children in the poorest countries the education they need to unlock their full potential. GPE’s mission is to mobilize global and national efforts to contribute to the achievement of equitable, quality education and learning for all, through inclusive partnership, a focus on effective and efficient education systems. Learn more: https://www.globalpartnership.org

Charities

Save The Children

For 100 years, Save The Children Foundation has been giving children in the U.S. and around the world a healthy start in life, the opportunity to learn and protection from harm. When crisis strikes, they are always among the first to respond and the last to leave. They do whatever it takes to save children, transforming their lives and the future they share. Learn more: https://www.savethechildren.org/
